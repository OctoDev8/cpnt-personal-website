Understanding Zen Philosophy

Gabriel Mejorada | Oct. 23, 2024

Emotional turmoil, stress and daily adversity piles up on us therefore what can we do to reduce extra blight that grinds us down? A complicated question that multiple philosophies try to answer but we will focus specifically on Zen philosophy for this blog.

Mindfulness

The concept of being fully present in the moment. Wholly focusing on the task or action that you are doing and not getting distracted by stressors or thoughts that have lingered for a long time is difficult to do. Zen's main aim is on being mindful, and taking the time to be grateful for simple moments that provide even the slightest respite from all the troubles of daily life.
